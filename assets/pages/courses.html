  • Cactus workshop

    • The idea of the workshop is to give some botanical and morphological characteristics of what cacti are.

  1. Insecticide workshop

    1. The Organic Insecticides Workshop was held in the community of Periquito. Organic insecticides can be prepared with vegetative materials that grow nearby and/or natural materials that can be easily obtained, therefore, their use has the advantage that the cost of the insecticide is very low, in addition to the impacts to both the environment and humans are minimal.
    2. The community members are very interested in this and there was a lot of participation from both the members of the irrigation association and the youth.

  2. Aquatic plants workshop

    1. Provide information on the relevant morphological characters to be taken into consideration for the knowledge and management of the different aquatic species for their cultivation and use in garden design.
    2. Deepen knowledge about the conservation of aquatic plants, training and disseminating the basic tools for successful cultivation and control of them.
